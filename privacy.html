Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Key Point: FaceHider AI processes all your photos and videos entirely on your device. Your media never leaves your phone.

1. Introduction

FaceHider AI ("the App") is developed by Eduard Koblyk ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. On-Device Processing

FaceHider AI is designed with a privacy-first architecture. All photo and video processing, including face detection, object recognition, and blur/mosaic effects, is performed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision Framework and Metal GPU acceleration.

  • Your photos and videos are never uploaded to any server.
  • Face detection and tracking runs locally using Apple's on-device AI.
  • No cloud processing or external APIs are used for media analysis.
  • Processed media is saved only to your device's Photo Library or shared via the iOS share sheet at your discretion.

3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to improve the app experience:

3.1 Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • App usage patterns (screens viewed, features used)
  • Device type and iOS version
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics

This data is anonymized, aggregated, and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3.2 Subscription Data

If you subscribe to FaceHider AI Premium, subscription management is handled by RevenueCat and Apple's App Store. We receive:

  • Subscription status (active/expired)
  • Subscription plan type
  • Anonymous subscriber identifier

We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple.

3.3 Photos and Videos

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your photos or videos. Access to your Photo Library is requested solely to allow you to select and save media within the app. This access is governed by iOS permissions that you control.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google) — Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store — Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to Firebase's default retention policies (typically 14 months). We do not maintain any personal data beyond what is described above. You can reset your analytics identifier by reinstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

FaceHider AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection (by limiting ad tracking in iOS Settings)
  • Revoke photo/camera permissions at any time via iOS Settings

8. Data Security

Since all media processing occurs on-device, your photos and videos are protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and biometric authentication. Network communications for analytics and subscription management use industry-standard TLS encryption.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
